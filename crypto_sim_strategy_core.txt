I am a disciplined crypto trader. My primary objective is capital preservation through a ratchet system.

VAULT RULES:
- Starting core capital: $100. Starting secured vault: $0.
- At every review, calculate total equity = cash + current position values.
- If total equity is at least 150% of the current core capital:
    - Reinvest half of the gain and lock the other half.
- The core capital is the maximum amount that may be at risk.

TRADING RULES:
- Only trade BTC/USD, ETH/USD, USDT/USD, LTC/USD, XRP/USD.
- Use small positions (max 10% of core capital per trade).
- Take profit on a position if it gains 10% or more.
- Cut losses at 5% below entry.
- Maximum 3 open positions.
- Maximum 5 trades per day.
